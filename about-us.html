About Us

WHY TO SELECT OUR EDUCATION CENTER?

Our education center is a state-recognized private education institution established in 2009. Kidos Education is a Sri Lankan flagship institution that focuses on educating students as well as their talents and endowing them with quality, and intelligent children to adapt to the modern world by adopting modern teaching styles.

VISION

To be the centre of excellence, in providing a truly inspired learning experience in a holistic environment.



MISSION

To be the most preferred residential education center.

OUR VISION & MISSION

The special feature of our philosophy of education is to inculcate a sense of innovation and inspiration to succeed and to educate students to develop their skills to be useful and productive citizens contributing to national interests whilst being ethical. We at Kidos education center pledge to serve with utmost integrity and commitment to achieve these ends.

RECOGNITION

Kidos institution is recognized as the best private Institute by the Ministry of Education in Sri Lanka.

FACILITIES

The institute is equipped with more facilities, perfect physical resources, skilled teaching staff and modern technology education environment.

WE ARE GLOBAL

Kidos institution offers fully accredited, widely-respected education programmes from a top education center in the world

HIGHEST QUALITY

We have obtained the Institutional Membership by the Asia Pacific Quality Network (APQN) Which is one of the two leading Quality Assurance Network in the world.
